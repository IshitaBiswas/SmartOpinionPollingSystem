Smart opinion is an Online opinion polling platform for the organizations to register themselves and post their questions/proposals on website for getting the public opinion which helps them to make their decisions in public interest. Our website provides a one stop platform for various sectors. Our project has many benefits for individuals, government bodies and organizations. Our website would provide a simple registration form where people can register to give their opinion with simple Like/Dislike with reasons in discussion board and also there would be a separate registration form for the organizations to post their questions/proposals/initiatives.
